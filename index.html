Let's go back to my PhD work for better understanding of my portfolio. During my PhD, I developed statistical models to analyse the data obtain from financial entities, ranging from year 1979-2014. The title of my thesis was Relationship of Macroeconomics variables on financial sector. I developed Econometric models, which predicted the behaviour of Economy through macroeconomic variables. For instance, In a layman's term, we all know that oil prices affects a state economy quite significantly. Sharp rise and fall in oil prices puts major stress over the financial entities of state. Through my designed Econometric models, the financial entities will have improved understiding of future events. In my thesis, I identified major variables, and by leveraging the advantage of probability theory and state-of-the-art Econometric solvers such as e-Views and STATA, I developed successful models that predicted the relationship of variables with more than 90% accuracy.
